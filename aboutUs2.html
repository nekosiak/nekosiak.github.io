About us

"Graphics" is a free online graphing calculator and place for mathematical calculations. The Desmos site offers powerful features for learning and performing graphical computing. You can enter mathematical expressions and get visual representation of the relationship between the input and output values of a mathematical function. Graphs of functions allow you to visually display the relationship between the argument (input value) and the value of the function (output value) on the coordinate plane.

Data charts are visual images used to present information and data in an understandable and accessible way. They allow you to visualize relationships, patterns, and trends in data, which helps you analyze information, draw conclusions, and make decisions. Also, there are Data Chart functions widely used in various fields including business, science, sociology, marketing, fiance and many others. They help understand market trends, map data structure, compare values, detect deviations, and make predictions.
